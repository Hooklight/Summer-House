What to Expect

Administration

Most peptides are administered via subcutaneous injection (similar to insulin injections), which we'll teach you to do comfortably at home. Some peptides are available in other forms like oral or nasal spray.

Timeline

Some benefits may be noticed within days to weeks, while others develop gradually over months. Peptide therapy often works best as part of a longer-term wellness strategy.

Safety

When prescribed and monitored by qualified medical professionals, peptide therapy is generally safe with minimal side effects. We source only high-quality, pharmaceutical-grade peptides.
