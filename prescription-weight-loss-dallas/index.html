Frequently Asked Questions

How much weight can I expect to lose with GLP-1 medications?
Most patients lose 15-20% of their body weight over 12-18 months with semaglutide or tirzepatide. For example, a 200 lb person typically loses 30-40 lbs. Results vary based on starting weight, medication choice, and lifestyle factors.
How much does prescription weight loss cost in Dallas?
Pricing varies based on the medication and whether you use brand-name or compounded versions. Compounded semaglutide typically ranges from $250-350/month, while brand-name options may be covered by insurance. We offer transparent pricing and membership options during your consultation.
What's the difference between semaglutide and tirzepatide?
Semaglutide (Wegovy, Ozempic) is a GLP-1 agonist that helps with appetite control and weight loss. Tirzepatide (Mounjaro, Zepbound) is a dual GLP-1/GIP agonist that often produces greater weight loss (20-22% vs 15-18% on average). Our medical team will help you choose the best option during your consultation.
Are there side effects?
Common side effects include nausea, mild digestive discomfort, and decreased appetite (which is therapeutic). Most side effects are mild and decrease over time. Our team monitors you closely and adjusts dosing to minimize side effects while maximizing results.
Do I need to be diabetic to get GLP-1 medication?
No. While GLP-1 medications were originally developed for diabetes, they are FDA-approved for weight loss in non-diabetic patients with a BMI of 30+ (or 27+ with weight-related health conditions). Our Dallas clinic specializes in weight loss treatment for all qualifying patients.
How long do I need to stay on the medication?
Most patients stay on GLP-1 medications for 12-18 months to achieve their weight loss goals. Some choose to continue long-term for weight maintenance. We'll create a personalized plan and can taper you off when appropriate.
Why choose a Dallas medical spa vs. online providers?
At Summer House Aesthetics, you receive in-person medical evaluations, lab work, ongoing monitoring, and same-day support from local Dallas providers. Online-only services lack comprehensive medical oversight, which can be dangerous for weight loss medications. Your safety is our priority.
Do you accept insurance?
We can provide documentation for insurance reimbursement, but most insurance plans don't cover GLP-1 for weight loss (only for diabetes). Our compounded medication options make treatment more affordable than brand-name drugs. We'll discuss all payment options during your consultation.
Where is Summer House Aesthetics located in Dallas?
We're located in Preston Hollow, North Dallas, near Highway 75. Convenient for patients from Uptown, Highland Park, Park Cities, Plano, Frisco, and throughout the Dallas metroplex. Private parking and discreet entrance available.
Will I gain the weight back after stopping?
Weight regain is possible if you return to previous eating habits. However, many patients maintain their weight loss by implementing lifestyle changes during treatment. We provide nutritional guidance and can create a maintenance plan, which may include a lower maintenance dose.
