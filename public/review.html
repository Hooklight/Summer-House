Section 02

The Brand System

A full brand system was designed for Summer House — not just colors and fonts, but a feeling. The brand brief describes it this way:

"Cape Cod coastal meets Michigan lake house. Nancy Meyers meets midwestern Ralph Lauren polo. The naturally beautiful girl who is stunning in an unassuming way. Golden retriever, green grass, clear skies, ice cream on a summer's night. A wedding band under a white tent late into the night. Fireworks on the beach."

"This site should make people exhale. It should feel like walking into a beautifully lit room where someone already poured you a glass of wine."

Design Principles (in priority order)

1. Breathing room over density. When in doubt, add more whitespace.

2. Warmth over precision. Soft edges, gentle shadows, organic shapes. Nothing clinical.

3. Photography over graphics. Every section should feel like a lifestyle magazine.

4. Restraint over impact. Whisper, don't shout. No "Book Now!" energy.

5. Texture over flat. Subtle grain, linen-like backgrounds, layered depth.

Section 03

The Domain Portfolio — 9 SEO Domains

Nine exact-match domains were purchased and built out with dedicated landing pages. The strategy is called "domain stacking" — owning the exact keywords people search for, then either hosting standalone landing pages or redirecting them to the main site's relevant service pages. This is one of the most powerful local SEO tactics available.

Why This Matters

When someone Googles "dallas filler" and the domain dallasfiller.com appears, it carries inherent authority. Google gives weight to exact-match domains for local searches. Combined with proper content, this portfolio gives Summer House a massive competitive advantage that would take years to replicate organically.

Peptide Therapy Content — 8 Condition Pages

Eight in-depth condition pages were written for the peptide therapy hub. These are designed as SEO "hub and spoke" content — a central peptide therapy page links out to condition-specific pages, each targeting different symptoms and search terms. The content is written in Dr. Daniel Kim's clinical voice, citing published research from journals like Gut, The Lancet, and Journal of Physiology and Pharmacology.

The 8 Condition Pages
  • Gut Health Peptides — BPC-157 for gut lining repair, IBS, bloating, leaky gut
  • Inflammation Peptide Therapy — Systemic inflammation, autoimmune-adjacent conditions
  • Joint Pain Peptide Therapy — Joint repair, mobility, recovery from chronic pain
  • Skin Rejuvenation Peptides — Collagen stimulation, skin texture, anti-aging
  • Post-Surgical Recovery — Accelerated healing after procedures
  • Stubborn Fat Peptide Therapy — Targeted fat reduction, body composition
  • Sleep Optimization Peptides — Sleep quality, circadian rhythm support
  • Sexual Health Peptide Therapy — Hormonal balance, libido, sexual wellness

Quality Standard: A detailed quality-pass process was designed to bring each page to a 93/100 score — content that reads like a confident clinical copywriter, not AI-generated text. The process includes eliminating AI-detectable patterns, injecting clinical voice, varying sentence structure, adding Dallas-specific details, and removing banned "AI" words (delve, leverage, robust, seamless, etc.).
