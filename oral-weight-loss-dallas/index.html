Frequently Asked Questions About Oral Weight Loss Medications

What's the difference between oral and injectable weight loss medications?
Oral GLP-1 (Rybelsus) is taken as a daily pill, while injectable GLP-1 (Wegovy/Ozempic) is a weekly injection. Injectable medications are significantly more effective (15-20% weight loss vs 5-8%), but oral options offer convenience for patients who prefer pills. Summer House will offer both options.
How much weight will I lose with oral medications?
Rybelsus typically produces 5-8% average weight loss, which is significant but less than injectable options. Results vary based on starting weight, dose, compliance, and lifestyle factors. Medical supervision helps optimize results.
When will oral GLP-1 be available at Summer House Dallas?
We're actively preparing to offer oral GLP-1 medications. Sign up for our notification list to be the first to know. You can also start with our proven injectable options available now.
Are there side effects with oral weight loss medications?
Common side effects include nausea, vomiting, constipation, and decreased appetite. Most side effects diminish within 2-4 weeks as your body adjusts. Our medical team helps manage side effects through dose adjustments and support.
How do I take Rybelsus?
Rybelsus is a dissolvable tablet placed under your tongue in the morning on an empty stomach. You must wait 30 minutes before eating or drinking anything other than water. This is one reason it's less convenient than injectable options.
Can I switch between oral and injectable?
Yes. Many patients start with one option and switch to another based on preference, tolerance, or results. Summer House supports both options and can help you transition if needed.
Is oral weight loss medication covered by insurance?
Coverage varies. Some insurance plans cover Rybelsus for diabetes, but oral GLP-1 for weight loss is rarely covered. Injectable GLP-1 coverage is also limited. Summer House can help you navigate insurance options and financing.
How much do oral weight loss medications cost?
Rybelsus typically costs $900-$1,400 per month without insurance, similar to injectable options. Summer House works with patients on financing options and may offer package pricing.
Who is a good candidate for oral weight loss medication?
Ideal candidates are those with BMI over 27 with health conditions, or BMI over 30, seeking weight loss with medical supervision. Our team evaluates your specific situation to determine the best option. A consultation with our medical professionals is the best way to determine suitability.
What makes Summer House different for weight loss?
Summer House offers comprehensive medical supervision, customized treatment plans, regular monitoring, nutritional guidance, and ongoing support. We're bringing both oral and injectable options to Dallas, so you can choose what works best for you.
