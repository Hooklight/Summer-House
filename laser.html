What to Expect

Before Treatment

  • Comprehensive skin consultation
  • Discussion of goals and concerns
  • Customized treatment plan
  • Avoid sun exposure 2 weeks prior
  • Discontinue certain skincare products

During Treatment

  • Treatment takes 30-60 minutes
  • Topical numbing for comfort
  • Cooling technology during treatment
  • Warm sensation or mild discomfort
  • Immediate return to activities

After Treatment

  • Mild redness and warmth (1-3 days)
  • Sun protection essential
  • Gentle skincare routine
  • Avoid heat and exercise 24-48 hours
  • Gradual improvement over weeks

Results & Timeline

  • Visible improvement after first treatment
  • Series of 3-6 treatments recommended
  • Treatments spaced 4-6 weeks apart
  • Cumulative, progressive results
  • Maintenance treatments as needed
