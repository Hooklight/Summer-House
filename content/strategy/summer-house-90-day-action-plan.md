This is your **roadmap for the first 90 days** of implementing the Summer House marketing strategy. It's designed to be:

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Issue #1: "I'm not ranking in the Map Pack"
**Solutions:**
- Keep building reviews (40-50+ needed to compete)
- Add more photos to GBP (20+ new photos/month)
- Post on GBP 2-3x/week
- Get more citations (aim for 50+ total)
- Ensure NAP consistency across all directories
- Add more service-specific content to GBP

### Issue #2: "I'm not getting enough leads"
**Solutions:**
- Check website speed (slow sites kill conversions)
- Add clear CTAs on every page ("Book Now," "Call Now")
- Make phone number click-to-call on mobile
- Add live chat or chatbot
- Simplify booking process (fewer steps = more bookings)
- Add trust signals (reviews, certifications, before/afters)

### Issue #3: "Social media isn't growing"
**Solutions:**
- Post more frequently (3-5x/week minimum)
- Use trending sounds on TikTok/Reels
- Engage more (reply to every comment, DM, mention)
- Collaborate with local influencers
- Use local hashtags (#DallasMedSpa, #UptownDallas, etc.)
- Be authentic (less polished, more real)

### Issue #4: "Paid ads aren't working"
**Solutions:**
- Check conversion tracking (is it set up correctly?)
- Review landing pages (are they clear and compelling?)
- Test different ad creatives (A/B test images, copy, CTAs)
- Adjust targeting (narrow or broaden audience)
- Pause low-performing campaigns (focus budget on winners)
- Give it time (need 2-4 weeks of data to optimize)

### Issue #5: "I don't have time for all this"
**Solutions:**
- Batch content creation (film 10 videos at once, schedule for a month)
- Outsource blog writing ($200-400/post)
- Hire VA for social media scheduling ($200-400/month)
- Use tools to automate (email sequences, social scheduling, review requests)
- Focus on 80/20 (which 20% of tasks drive 80% of results? Do those.)
