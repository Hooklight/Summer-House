# Summer House Aesthetics - Content Marketing Plan
## Dallas, TX Medical Spa

---

## Executive Summary

This content marketing plan is designed to:
1. **Position Summer House as the trusted authority** for aesthetic treatments in Dallas
2. **Drive local SEO rankings** through optimized blog content
3. **Build social media presence** on Instagram and TikTok
4. **Convert followers into patients** through strategic nurture campaigns
5. **Require minimal time commitment** (2-4 hours/week once systems are in place)

**Brand Voice:** Warm, elevated, approachable, knowledgeable but not clinical.  
Think: Your educated best friend who happens to be an aesthetic expert.

### Filming Tips for Jessica

**Keep It Simple:**
- Use your iPhone (no fancy equipment needed)
- Natural lighting (near a window) > ring light
- Simple background (clean wall, office space)
- Free editing apps: CapCut, InShot, Canva

**Batch Film:**
- Set aside 1 hour once a week
- Film 5-10 videos in one session
- Post them over the next 1-2 weeks
- Takes pressure off daily content creation

**Be Authentic:**
- You don't need to be "perfect" on camera
- Authentic > polished on social media
- Show your personality (warm, knowledgeable, approachable)
- It's okay to stumble or laugh — keep it real
