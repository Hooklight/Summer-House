## 4. Review Generation Strategy

### Why Reviews Matter
- **58% of consumers** visit a business that responds to reviews
- **4+ star average** needed to stay competitive
- **Recency matters:** Fresh reviews signal an active business
- **Keywords in reviews** help with ranking (patients naturally mention "Botox," "semaglutide," etc.)

### Current State Assessment
- [ ] How many Google reviews do you currently have? _______
- [ ] Average star rating? _______
- [ ] Most recent review date? _______
- [ ] How many reviews on Yelp? _______
- [ ] How many on RealSelf? _______

**Goal:** 50+ Google reviews with 4.8+ average within 6 months.

### Review Request System (Repeatable Process)

#### TIMING: When to Ask
**Best times:**
1. **Immediately after treatment** (when patient is happy and present)
2. **2-7 days post-treatment** (via text/email when results are visible)
3. **After a positive interaction** (patient gives verbal compliment)

**Don't ask:**
- If something went wrong
- If patient seems unhappy or hesitant
- If it's their first visit and results aren't visible yet

#### METHOD 1: In-Person Ask (Highest Conversion ~30%)
**Script for front desk/Jessica:**
"We're so glad you had a great experience! Would you mind taking 60 seconds to leave us a quick Google review? It really helps other people find us. I can text you the link right now."

- Use a **Google review short link** (create at g.page/[your-business]/review)
- Text it immediately while they're still there
- Make it easy: "Just click, rate, and write one sentence"

#### METHOD 2: Automated Text/Email (Conversion ~10-15%)
Use automated follow-up (via EHR system, Podium, Birdeye, or simple automation):

**Text message template (48 hours post-treatment):**
"Hi [Name]! It's Jessica from Summer House Aesthetics. I hope you're loving your [treatment]! If you have 60 seconds, we'd be so grateful for a Google review: [link]. Thanks for trusting us with your care! ✨"

**Email template (3-5 days post-treatment):**
Subject: "How are you feeling, [Name]?"

Hi [Name],

I wanted to check in and see how you're feeling after your [treatment] on [date]! 

If you're happy with your results (and we hope you are!), would you take a moment to share your experience on Google? Your feedback helps other people find us and means the world to our small business.

[BUTTON: Leave a Google Review]

Thank you for choosing Summer House Aesthetics!

Warmly,  
Jessica

#### METHOD 3: Review Incentive Program (Use Carefully)
**What you CAN do:**
- Enter reviewers into a monthly drawing (e.g., $100 product credit)
- Offer a "refer a friend" discount (doesn't require review)
- Give a small thank-you gift ($10-20 value) for feedback (not explicitly for positive reviews)

**What you CANNOT do:**
- Pay for reviews
- Offer discounts in exchange for reviews
- Only give incentives for 5-star reviews

### Responding to Reviews (CRITICAL)

**Respond to EVERY review within 24-48 hours.**

#### Positive Reviews (5 stars)
**Template:**
"Thank you so much, [Name]! We're thrilled you loved your [treatment]. It's always a joy working with patients who trust us with their aesthetic goals. Can't wait to see you again soon! ✨ – Jessica & the Summer House team"

**Tips:**
- Personalize (mention their treatment)
- Keep it warm and brief
- Reinforce brand personality
- Don't sound robotic

#### Negative Reviews (1-3 stars)
**Template:**
"Hi [Name], thank you for sharing your feedback. I'm sorry your experience didn't meet expectations. We'd love to make this right – please reach out to us directly at [phone/email] so we can discuss. We're committed to providing the best possible care. – Jessica"

**Tips:**
- Never argue or get defensive
- Take it offline quickly
- Show empathy
- Demonstrate you care about resolving it

#### Neutral Reviews (3-4 stars)
**Template:**
"Thanks for your review, [Name]! We appreciate your feedback and are always looking to improve. If there's anything we can do to make your next visit even better, please don't hesitate to reach out. – Jessica"

### Review Monitoring Tools

## 6. Link Building Strategy (Advanced - Month 3+)

### Why Backlinks Matter
Google sees links from other websites as "votes of confidence." More quality links = higher rankings.

### Local Link Opportunities

**Easy Wins:**
1. **Local business associations:** Dallas Chamber of Commerce, local business improvement districts
2. **Supplier/partner links:** Your laser equipment company, product brands you use (Allergan, etc.)
3. **Press releases:** Local news sites when you launch new services
4. **Sponsorships:** Local charity events, school fundraisers (get link on their sponsor page)
5. **Guest appearances:** Local podcasts, interviews, blogs
6. **Professional directories:** Medical associations, aesthetic societies

**Content-Based Links:**
1. Write expert guides → reach out to Dallas lifestyle blogs to feature them
2. Offer expert quotes to journalists (use HARO - Help A Reporter Out)
3. Partner with local influencers (they link to you from blog/link-in-bio)
4. Create shareable infographics about treatments
5. Host events and get covered by local media

**Strategic Partnerships:**
1. Collaborate with complementary businesses (yoga studios, wellness centers, nutritionists)
2. Cross-promote with non-competing med spas in other cities
3. Get featured in local "best of" lists
