# Summer House Aesthetics - Paid Media Strategy
## Dallas, TX Medical Spa

---

## Executive Summary

Paid media should complement (not replace) your organic strategy. The goal is to **accelerate patient acquisition** while building long-term organic assets.

**Key Philosophy:**
- Start small, test, scale what works
- Focus on high-intent, ready-to-book traffic first
- Track ROI obsessively (every dollar must be measurable)
- Prioritize platforms with best med spa performance

**Recommended Budget:** $1,000-2,500/month to start (scalable)

## 1. Platform Priority & Strategy

### PRIORITY 1: Google Local Service Ads (RECOMMENDED)

#### What Are Local Service Ads (LSAs)?
- Appear ABOVE Google Map Pack and organic results
- "Google Guaranteed" badge (trust signal)
- Pay-per-lead (not per click) — only pay when someone contacts you
- Perfect for high-intent, local searches

#### Why LSAs Are Perfect for Med Spas
✅ **High-intent traffic** (people actively searching "med spa near me")  
✅ **Pay only for qualified leads** (calls, messages, bookings)  
✅ **Google Guaranteed badge** (builds trust)  
✅ **Appear above competitors** (top of search results)  
✅ **Cost-effective** (typically $15-40 per lead)  

#### How to Set Up LSAs
1. **Apply for Google Guaranteed** (verification process)
   - Business license
   - Insurance documentation
   - Background checks (for Jessica)
2. **Create LSA profile** (similar to Google Business Profile)
3. **Set weekly budget** (start with $250-500/week)
4. **Choose service areas** (Dallas + target neighborhoods)
5. **Select services** (medical spa, weight loss, Botox, laser, etc.)
6. **Track leads** (calls and messages come through Google's system)

#### Expected Results
- **Cost per lead:** $20-50 (varies by competition)
- **Lead quality:** High (actively searching for your services)
- **Conversion rate:** 20-30% (consultation bookings from leads)
- **ROI:** 3-5x (if services are $500-2000)

#### Example Budget & ROI
**Monthly Budget:** $1,500  
**Leads Generated:** 30-50 leads/month  
**Consultation Bookings:** 10-15 consultations  
**Patient Conversions:** 5-8 new patients  
**Average Treatment Value:** $1,000  
**Revenue:** $5,000-8,000  
**ROI:** 3-5x  

**Recommendation:** START HERE. LSAs should be your first paid investment.

### PRIORITY 2: Google Ads (Search Campaigns)

#### What Are Google Search Ads?
- Text ads that appear at the top of Google search results
- Pay-per-click (PPC) model
- Target specific keywords (e.g., "semaglutide Dallas")
- Highly customizable

#### Why Google Ads Work for Med Spas
✅ **Target high-intent keywords** (people searching for treatments NOW)  
✅ **Control costs** (set max bid, daily budget)  
✅ **Fast results** (start getting traffic immediately)  
✅ **Measurable** (track clicks, conversions, ROI precisely)  
✅ **Scalable** (increase budget as you see success)

### PRIORITY 3: Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)

#### What Are Meta Ads?
- Visual ads on Facebook and Instagram feeds, stories, and reels
- Target by demographics, interests, behaviors, location
- Great for awareness and consideration (not as high-intent as Google)

#### Why Meta Ads Work for Med Spas
✅ **Visual platform** (before/afters, treatment videos)  
✅ **Hyper-targeted** (age, location, interests)  
✅ **Awareness + retargeting** (stay top-of-mind)  
✅ **Lower cost per click** (vs. Google Ads)  
✅ **Builds social proof** (engagement, followers, brand awareness)  

#### Recommended Campaign Structure

**CAMPAIGN 1: Awareness (Top of Funnel)**
- **Goal:** Introduce Summer House to Dallas audience
- **Ad Type:** Video or carousel
- **Targeting:**
  - Women 28-55
  - Dallas metro (or specific zip codes: Highland Park, Uptown, Park Cities)
  - Interests: Beauty, wellness, weight loss, fitness, med spas
- **Creative:**
  - "Meet Summer House Aesthetics: Dallas's Modern Med Spa"
  - Showcase office, team, treatments
  - Warm, inviting video tour
- **Budget:** $300-500/month
- **KPI:** Cost per 1,000 impressions (CPM), video views, engagement

**CAMPAIGN 2: Lead Generation (Middle of Funnel)**
- **Goal:** Capture leads for consultations
- **Ad Type:** Lead form ads (users fill out form without leaving Facebook/Instagram)
- **Targeting:**
  - Women 28-55
  - Dallas metro
  - Interests: Weight loss, GLP-1, Botox, aesthetic treatments
- **Creative:**
  - "Book Your Free Consultation at Summer House Aesthetics"
  - Before/after images
  - Special offer: "$50 off your first treatment"
- **Budget:** $400-700/month
- **KPI:** Cost per lead ($10-25)

**CAMPAIGN 3: Retargeting (Bottom of Funnel)**
- **Goal:** Convert website visitors who didn't book
- **Ad Type:** Image or carousel ads
- **Targeting:**
  - People who visited your website (install Meta Pixel)
  - People who engaged with your Instagram/Facebook
- **Creative:**
  - "Still thinking about [treatment]? Book now and save $50"
  - Patient testimonials
  - Limited-time offers
- **Budget:** $200-400/month
- **KPI:** Conversion rate, cost per booking

**CAMPAIGN 4: GLP-1 Weight Loss Specific**
- **Goal:** Capture GLP-1 demand specifically
- **Ad Type:** Video or before/after carousel
- **Targeting:**
  - Women 30-55
  - Dallas metro
  - Interests: Weight loss, GLP-1, semaglutide, wellness
- **Creative:**
  - "Lose Weight with Medical Supervision: GLP-1 at Summer House"
  - Real patient results (with permission)
  - Testimonial videos
- **Budget:** $400-600/month
- **KPI:** Cost per lead, consultation bookings

#### Creative Best Practices

**DO:**
- Use real before/after photos (with patient permission)
- Show Jessica/team (builds trust and personality)
- Include clear CTA ("Book Now," "Learn More," "Get $50 Off")
- Use video (higher engagement than static images)
- Test multiple creatives (A/B test)

**DON'T:**
- Use stock photos (feels generic)
- Overpromise results ("Lose 50 lbs in 30 days")
- Violate Facebook's ad policies (before/afters are allowed, but must be realistic)
- Use too much text on images (Facebook penalizes this)

#### Expected Results (Meta Ads)
**Monthly Budget:** $1,000-1,500  
**Impressions:** 50,000-100,000  
**Clicks:** 300-600  
**Leads:** 30-60  
**Consultation Bookings:** 10-20  
**New Patients:** 5-10  
**Average Treatment Value:** $800-1,500  
**Revenue:** $4,000-15,000  
**ROI:** 2-10x (varies by creative and targeting)  

**Recommendation:** Start with lead gen + GLP-1 campaigns. Add awareness + retargeting after 2-3 months.

- Video ads on TikTok's "For You" page

**Start with organic TikTok first** (see Content Marketing Plan).  
**Add paid after you have organic traction** (3-6 months in).

**Campaign Type:**
- **In-Feed Ads** (videos that appear in For You page)
- **Spark Ads** (boost your own organic TikToks)

**Targeting:**
- Women 25-45
- Dallas metro
- Interests: Beauty, wellness, self-care, weight loss

**Creative:**
- Authentic, not overly produced
- "Day in the life," "Get ready with me," transformation stories
- Use trending sounds and formats

**Budget:** $500-1,000/month (test)

**Recommendation:** WAIT on TikTok ads until organic TikTok is performing. Focus on Google + Meta first.

## 3. Tracking & Measurement

### Essential Setup (DO THIS BEFORE SPENDING A DOLLAR)

#### 1. Google Analytics 4 (GA4)
- Track website traffic sources (Google Ads, Meta, organic, etc.)
- Set up conversion tracking:
  - Consultation form submissions
  - Phone calls (via call tracking)
  - Online bookings

#### 2. Call Tracking
- Use a service like CallRail or CallTrackingMetrics
- Assign unique phone numbers to each ad platform
- Track which ads drive phone calls

#### 3. Meta Pixel
- Install on website
- Track visitors from Facebook/Instagram
- Enable retargeting campaigns
- Measure conversions

#### 4. Google Tag Manager
- Centralize all tracking codes
- Easier to manage vs. hardcoding scripts

#### 5. CRM or Spreadsheet
- Track every lead:
  - Source (Google, Meta, organic, referral)
  - Date
  - Contact info
  - Status (consultation booked, patient, no-show, etc.)
  - Revenue generated
- Calculate ROI by source

---

### Key Metrics to Track

**For Every Platform:**
- **Spend:** How much did you invest?
- **Impressions:** How many people saw the ad?
- **Clicks:** How many clicked?
- **CTR (Click-Through Rate):** Clicks ÷ Impressions (higher = better)
- **CPC (Cost Per Click):** Spend ÷ Clicks (lower = better)
- **Leads:** How many contacted you?
- **CPL (Cost Per Lead):** Spend ÷ Leads (lower = better)
- **Conversions:** How many booked consultations?
- **Patients:** How many became paying patients?
- **Revenue:** How much did they spend?
- **ROI:** (Revenue - Spend) ÷ Spend (higher = better)

## 6. Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Mistake #1: Not Tracking Conversions
**Problem:** You're spending money but don't know what's working.  
**Solution:** Set up GA4, call tracking, and lead tracking from day one.

### ❌ Mistake #2: Sending Ads to Homepage
**Problem:** Generic homepage doesn't convert (too many options).  
**Solution:** Send each ad to a specific landing page (GLP-1 ad → GLP-1 page).

### ❌ Mistake #3: No Clear CTA
**Problem:** Visitors don't know what to do next.  
**Solution:** Every ad and landing page needs ONE clear CTA ("Book Consultation," "Call Now," "Get $50 Off").

### ❌ Mistake #4: Ignoring Mobile Experience
**Problem:** 70%+ of traffic is mobile, but your site is desktop-focused.  
**Solution:** Ensure mobile-friendly site, click-to-call buttons, easy booking.

### ❌ Mistake #5: "Set It and Forget It"
**Problem:** Ads need constant optimization.  
**Solution:** Review performance weekly for first 2 months, then biweekly.

### ❌ Mistake #6: Spreading Budget Too Thin
**Problem:** Trying to be on every platform with tiny budgets.  
**Solution:** Start with 1-2 platforms, master them, then expand.

### ❌ Mistake #7: Competing on Price
**Problem:** "Cheap Botox!" attracts price-shoppers (bad patients).  
**Solution:** Compete on value, trust, expertise (not just price).

## 7. When Paid Ads Make Sense (and When They Don't)

### ✅ **INVEST IN PAID ADS IF:**
- You have a proven service offering (you've treated patients successfully)
- You can handle the patient volume (you have capacity)
- You're tracking conversions properly (you know your ROI)
- Your website is optimized (fast, mobile-friendly, clear CTAs)
- You have at least 20+ Google reviews (social proof)
- Your budget allows for $1,000+/month (minimum for meaningful results)

### ❌ **DON'T INVEST IN PAID ADS IF:**
- You don't have conversion tracking set up
- Your website is slow, broken, or unclear
- You don't have social proof (reviews, testimonials)
- You can't handle more patients (booked solid)
- Your budget is under $500/month (too small to see results)

**Priority Order:**
1. **Fix the fundamentals** (website, GBP, reviews)
2. **Build organic content** (SEO, social media)
3. **THEN invest in paid ads** (accelerate growth)
