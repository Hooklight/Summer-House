Frequently Asked Questions

How much weight can I expect to lose?

Clinical trials show average weight loss of 15-22% of total body weight over 6-12 months. Individual results vary based on starting weight, adherence to the program, lifestyle factors, and medication used.

Is the injection painful?

The needles are very small and thin. Most patients report minimal discomfort, describing it as a small pinch. We provide thorough training to ensure you're comfortable administering injections at home.

Will I regain weight when I stop the medication?

Some weight regain is possible after stopping GLP-1 therapy. However, the lifestyle changes you develop during treatment, combined with our maintenance strategies, help many patients sustain their weight loss long-term.

How long do I need to take the medication?

Treatment duration varies by individual. Most patients continue for 6-12 months to reach their goal weight, then work with our team on a maintenance plan which may include a lower dose or transitioning off medication.

Does insurance cover GLP-1 weight loss medications?

Insurance coverage varies widely. Many plans do not cover weight loss medications. We offer competitive self-pay pricing and can provide documentation for you to submit to your insurance if desired.

Can I exercise while on GLP-1 medication?

Absolutely! We encourage regular physical activity as it enhances weight loss results and overall health. Start gradually and listen to your body, especially in the first few weeks as you adjust to the medication.
