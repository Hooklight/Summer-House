Frequently Asked Questions

Does Botox hurt?

Most patients describe the sensation as a small pinch or mosquito bite. The needles are very fine, and treatment is quick. We can apply topical numbing if you're sensitive to injections.

When will I see results?

You'll begin to notice improvement within 3-5 days, with full results visible at 10-14 days. This is why we schedule follow-up assessments at two weeks.

Will I look frozen or unnatural?

Not when done properly. Our goal is natural-looking results that preserve your expressions. You'll look refreshed, not frozen. The "overdone" look comes from excessive dosing, which we avoid.

How long does Botox last?

Results typically last 3-4 months. Some areas (like crow's feet) may last longer, while areas of strong muscle movement (like forehead) may need more frequent touch-ups. With regular treatments, you may find the results last longer over time.

What's the difference between Botox, Jeuveau, and Xeomin?

All three are neuromodulators that work similarly by relaxing facial muscles. The main differences are in formulation and manufacturing. Results are comparable across all three products when administered by an experienced injector.

When should I start getting Botox?

There's no specific "right age" to start. Some people begin in their late 20s for prevention, while others wait until lines are more established. During consultation, we'll assess your skin and recommend a timeline that makes sense for you.

Are there any side effects?

Side effects are typically minimal and temporary. You may experience slight bruising, redness, or mild headache. Serious side effects are rare when treatments are performed by qualified providers.
