Understanding Oral GLP-1 (Rybelsus)

Rybelsus is currently the only FDA-approved oral GLP-1 medication available in the United States. It offers a pill-based alternative to injectable GLP-1 medications like Ozempic and Wegovy.

How Rybelsus Works

Rybelsus (oral semaglutide) works by mimicking the GLP-1 hormone, which reduces appetite, increases feelings of fullness, and helps regulate blood sugar. The medication comes as a dissolvable tablet placed under the tongue for rapid absorption.

Rybelsus Dosing in Dallas:
• Available doses: 3mg, 7mg, and 14mg
• Typical Dallas starting dose: 3mg daily
• Dose increases based on tolerance and response
• Must be taken on empty stomach (30 min before food/drink)

Expected Results with Oral GLP-1

Clinical studies show modest but consistent weight loss with Rybelsus:

  • 3mg dose: Average 3-5% weight loss
  • 7mg dose: Average 5-7% weight loss
  • 14mg dose: Average 5-8% weight loss

For comparison: Injectable GLP-1 (Wegovy/Ozempic) produces 15-20% average weight loss, making it significantly more effective but requiring weekly injections.

How much weight will I lose with oral GLP-1?
Rybelsus typically produces 5-8% average weight loss. Results vary based on starting weight, dose, lifestyle factors, and individual response. This is less dramatic than injectable GLP-1 (15-20%) but still significant.
Can I switch from oral to injectable GLP-1?
Yes. Many patients start with oral for convenience and switch to injectable if they want more aggressive weight loss. Summer House supports both options and can help you transition if needed.
What are the side effects of oral GLP-1?
Common side effects include nausea, vomiting, constipation, and appetite suppression. Most side effects diminish within 2-4 weeks as your body adjusts. Our medical team helps manage any side effects.
Why does Rybelsus have lower weight loss than injectable?
Oral medications have lower bioavailability—only 1-2% of the active drug is absorbed compared to near 100% with injections. This means less drug reaches your system, resulting in lower appetite suppression and weight loss.
Do I need to take Rybelsus on an empty stomach?
Yes. Rybelsus must be taken on an empty stomach and you must wait 30 minutes before eating or drinking anything other than water. This requirement makes it less flexible than injectable options.
Is oral GLP-1 covered by insurance in Dallas?
Insurance coverage varies. Some plans cover Rybelsus when prescribed for diabetes management, but oral GLP-1 for weight loss is rarely covered. We can help you navigate insurance options.
Will Summer House help if I'm currently on injectable GLP-1?
Absolutely. If you're already getting results with Wegovy or Ozempic, we can continue your care. If you want to try oral options or explore other treatments, we're here to help.
