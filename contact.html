Before Your Visit

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring a valid ID and any relevant medical records or current medications list. If you're a new patient, arrive 10 minutes early to complete paperwork.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer complimentary consultations for most aesthetic treatments. This allows us to assess your needs and create a personalized treatment plan with no obligation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, HSA/FSA cards, and cash. We also offer flexible payment plans for certain treatments.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee.

Do you take insurance?

Most aesthetic treatments are considered elective and are not covered by insurance. However, some treatments like medical weight loss may be covered depending on your plan. We can provide documentation for you to submit to your insurance.
